Defining Moments in Foreign Policy
The Naval Issue (1909-1910)Britain need countries’ help to buy newer ships
-Imperialists (English)
-Yes, pay to build new battleships
-Nationalist (French)
-No, it does not affect Canada
Resolution
-None happy
-Laurier established the Royal Canadian Navy, which could be used by the british in case of emergency war
-Imperialists (English):”Tin pot navy”
-Nationalist (French):Too much
The Boer War (1899-1902): British was going to war with Afrikaans in South Africa and requested soldier to help them fight
-English
-Agreed to fight, 23, 000 signatures
-French
-Disagreed to fight
Resolution
-Laurier sent 1000 volunteers
